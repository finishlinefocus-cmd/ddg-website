Common residential garage door problems
These are the most common issues homeowners call about. If yours is not listed, send a photo or call and we will help figure it out.
🌀

Broken or Worn Springs

Springs break without warning and make the door suddenly heavy or unbalanced. This is one of the most common urgent repairs.

⚡

Opener Not Working

Remote, keypad, or wall button not responding — opener issues can usually be diagnosed and resolved quickly.

📐

Door Off Track

An off-track door is a safety concern and should not be forced. We can help get it back in place properly.

🚫

Door Won't Open or Close

Whether stuck open or stuck closed, we can help identify the issue and get your door operational.

🔊

Noisy or Rough Operation

Grinding, rattling, or rough movement often signals worn rollers, hardware, or lubrication issues — easier to address before they worsen.

🏠

Replacement Estimate

Need a new door? DDG can review options and provide a replacement estimate for residential garage doors.
