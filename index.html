About DDG

Commercial & Residential Specialists

Doors Docks and Gates, LLC is a locally owned and operated service company based in Rocky Face, Georgia. We serve residential homeowners and commercial facilities across Northwest Georgia, Tennessee, and surrounding areas.

Our technicians are I.D.A. certified and experienced with all major brands of doors, openers, dock equipment, and gate systems. We offer planned maintenance programs for commercial clients and same-day service when you need it most.

  • I.D.A. Certified Dock Door & Equipment Technicians
  • Ability to Service All Major Brands
  • Free Dock Surveys & Estimates
  • Planned Commercial Door Maintenance Programs
  • 24/7 Emergency Dock Door Repair
  • Fully Insured — Residential & Commercial
